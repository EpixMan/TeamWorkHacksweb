Privacy Policy for Add-ons

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy. The add-ons available on this website strictly adhere to privacy guidelines designed to protect user data. Below is an outline of our practices:

Data Collection and Usage

Our add-ons do not collect, rent, use, or share any user information in any way that compromises your privacy. However, some add-ons may require access to certain Google services to perform their intended functions.

Google Account Data

Any data that is collected through the add-ons remains within your Google account and is not shared outside of it unless required for the functionality of the add-on. We do not store your data on our servers.

Data Disclosure to OpenAI

In certain cases, data may be sent to OpenAI only when required for the functioning of our add-ons, such as utilizing AI-powered features. We will ensure that any such data exchange is minimal, relevant, and in full compliance with privacy guidelines.

Removal of Add-ons

Should you decide at any time to stop using our add-ons, you can easily remove them from your Google app. This action will stop any further access or data interactions by the add-ons.

Important Notice: We take user privacy seriously and strive to offer the best protection for your information. If you have any concerns about how your data is handled, feel free to contact us.
